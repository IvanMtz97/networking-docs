# Goal
Allow people with different talents connect with each other in order to build products and services.

## Example 1
- Person A is a very talented designer but doesnt have any business, sales or marketing experience and he uses the platform in order to find business partners in order to build a product or service.
- Person B is a very talented marketer but doesnt have any business, sales or marketing experience and he uses the platform in order to find business partners in order to build a product or service.
- Person C (Business) and Person D (Sales) are business owners and they use the platform in order to find business partners (experts) in order to improve their business.

The main goal of the platform is for talented people with a desire to have his own business/product/service by proposing a [Project](./glossary.md) so people navigating through the platform published projects can connect with the person proposing the project in order to build a product or service.
